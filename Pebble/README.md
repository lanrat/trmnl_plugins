# [Pebble Appstore](https://trmnl.com/recipes/281436?ref=mrlanrat)

Browse watchfaces and apps from the [repebble appstore](https://apps.repebble.com/) on your TRMNL device.

![Screenshot](screenshot.png)

## Description

Shows a randomized grid of items from the repebble appstore — Pebble's community-run replacement for the original Pebble app store. Pebble's 144x168 1-bit screenshots render beautifully on TRMNL's e-ink display.

## Settings

- **Feed** — `most-loved` (top picks by hearts) or `all` (every app)
- **Sort** — when Feed is `all`: `updated` (recently updated) or `released` (recently released)
- **Content Type** — `both`, `watchfaces`, or `apps`
